Gen X Edition

A search app to help you remember the good old days (if you're a member of Generation X) or to see how things were back then (if you're a Millennial). The Harvard Center defines Gen X'ers as people born between 1965 to 1984. Read Wikipedia articles and see the top Youtube search results relevant to your chosen year. Click start to begin!
